What is Full-Stack Development?

This industry defined buzz-word can be somewhat vague but that hasn't prevented it from catching on like wildfire. As a progressive approach to software development - Full-Stack Development requires one to have detailed knowledge of the entire Software / Solution Stack. Thus requiring proficiency in a variety of languages & frameworks.

The term 'Full-Stack' refers to the stack of multiple technologies that configures, compiles, and ultimatley serves up your application. Having the experience and knowledge of multiple languages, frameworks, SDKs / APIs, and the latest coding standards & best practices.

This does NOT imply that a full-stack developer has mastered everything within the overall SDLC; it DOES imply however, that he or she has the skills needed to weave in / out of each layer of the Stack comfortably with minimal assistance.
