What is "Full-Stack" Development?

A progressive approach to software development that requires the developer to have detailed knowledge of the Software / Solution Stack.

A full-stack developer is a person who is comfortable working in the front-end and back-end side of web development. This doesn't mean you have mastered everything within the overall SDLC; it means that you can work on both the front-end and back-end, with minimal assistance.
